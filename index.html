Alexey Molchanov

The deepest man in the world

A timeline of Alexey's major freediving achievements:

  • 1987 - Born in Volgograd, Russia
  • 2008 - First world record with a 250m Dynamic Apnea using a monofin.
  • 2012 - World record for depth with a 126m dive at Dean's Blue Hole in the Bahamas.
  • 2013 - Broke his own world record for depth with a 128m in Kalamata, Greece.
  • 2016 - Extends his record to 129m in Baja, California.
  • 2018 - Alexey dives to an astounting 130m in the Bahamas.
  • 2020 - Pushed his limits, and earned a Guinness World Record for longest recorded dynamic dive under ice at 181 meters.
  • 2021 - Alexey dove 80 meters beneath a one-meter-thick ice surface and back up on a single breath breaking another world record: the deepest free dive under the ice with fins.
  • 2022 - The legend continues...
